Services

Residential cleaning services for every main area of the home.

Shine Time Cleaners provides a wide range of residential cleaning services for houses and living spaces. Service can be scheduled as routine upkeep, a deeper reset, move-related cleaning, or a custom plan based on household priorities.

Standard Cleaning

Standard cleaning is designed for routine maintenance. It helps keep visible surfaces, floors, kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, and common areas feeling clean between deeper appointments. This service is a good fit for homes that are already in generally manageable condition and need dependable upkeep.

  • Dusting accessible surfaces, ledges, and furniture.
  • Cleaning kitchen counters, sinks, appliance exteriors, and tables.
  • Cleaning bathroom fixtures, mirrors, vanities, toilets, tubs, and showers.
  • Vacuuming carpets and rugs and mopping hard floors.

Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning provides a more detailed reset for homes that need extra attention. It is often useful before starting recurring service, after a busy season, before hosting, or when dust, buildup, and overlooked corners have accumulated. Deep cleaning may require more time because it addresses detail work beyond a typical maintenance visit.

  • More detailed attention to edges, baseboards, fixtures, and reachable buildup.
  • Additional focus on high-use rooms such as kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Careful dusting of accessible decor, shelves, and surface transitions.
  • Thorough floor care matched to the surfaces in the home.

Kitchen Cleaning

The kitchen is one of the hardest-working rooms in a home. Shine Time Cleaners focuses on the surfaces and touch points that make the kitchen feel brighter and easier to use, including counters, sinks, stovetop areas, appliance exteriors, cabinet fronts, tables, and floors.

  • Wipe counters, backsplashes, tables, and accessible cabinet fronts.
  • Clean sink basins, faucets, and visible appliance exteriors.
  • Address crumbs, splashes, fingerprints, and food-prep residue.
  • Vacuum and mop kitchen floors using appropriate supplies.

Bathroom Cleaning

Bathrooms benefit from consistent, detail-oriented cleaning. Service typically includes sinks, counters, mirrors, toilets, tubs, showers, fixtures, reachable shelves, and floors. The goal is a fresher space with cleaner surfaces and reduced visible buildup.

  • Clean and polish mirrors, faucets, sinks, and counters.
  • Clean toilets, tubs, showers, and exterior surfaces.
  • Wipe accessible shelving, ledges, and high-touch areas.
  • Vacuum and mop bathroom floors.

Bedroom Cleaning

Bedroom cleaning supports a more restful environment by reducing dust, refreshing floors, and tidying accessible surfaces. Depending on the agreed scope, cleaning can include dusting furniture, wiping touch points, vacuuming, mopping hard floors, and light straightening.

  • Dust nightstands, dressers, lamps, shelves, and accessible decor.
  • Vacuum carpets and rugs or mop hard flooring.
  • Wipe reachable high-touch surfaces and visible dust lines.
  • Support light room reset within the agreed cleaning scope.

Living Areas

Living rooms, family rooms, dining rooms, entryways, and hallways collect dust, footprints, crumbs, and fingerprints quickly. Shine Time Cleaners provides surface care and floor cleaning that helps shared spaces feel comfortable and ready for everyday use.

  • Dust furniture, reachable shelving, tables, and decorative surfaces.
  • Clean visible fingerprints on accessible surfaces and common touch points.
  • Vacuum soft flooring and rugs and mop hard floors.
  • Refresh entryways and hallways that carry dirt through the home.

Dusting

Dusting is more than a cosmetic task. Regular dust removal can make surfaces look brighter and help prevent buildup around furniture, ledges, shelves, electronics, and decorative items. Shine Time Cleaners dusts accessible surfaces with attention to room layout and surface type.

  • Dust accessible furniture, ledges, shelves, lamps, and decor.
  • Pay attention to baseboards and edges when included in the service scope.
  • Use suitable supplies for common household surfaces.
  • Prioritize visible dust patterns in frequently used rooms.

Vacuuming

Vacuuming helps remove dirt, dust, crumbs, and tracked-in debris from carpets, area rugs, and soft flooring. It is included in many cleaning plans and can be emphasized in homes with high foot traffic or specific rooms that collect more debris.

  • Vacuum carpets, rugs, bedrooms, hallways, and living areas.
  • Focus on traffic paths, room edges, and visible debris.
  • Coordinate with mopping where flooring transitions between rooms.
  • Adapt the routine to the home size and agreed service level.

Mopping

Mopping brings a finished feel to kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, laundry areas, and other hard floors. Shine Time Cleaners uses supplies appropriate for typical residential cleaning and can discuss special surface instructions before service begins.

  • Mop kitchens, bathrooms, entryways, and hard-surface living areas.
  • Address sticky spots, tracked-in dirt, and visible dullness.
  • Coordinate with vacuuming or sweeping for a cleaner result.
  • Respect special notes for floors that need particular care.

Move-in/Move-out Cleaning

Move-related cleaning helps prepare a home for arrival, departure, listing, or turnover. Because move cleaning can vary widely, the final scope depends on whether the home is empty, partly furnished, recently occupied, or in need of detailed attention.

  • Clean kitchens, bathrooms, floors, surfaces, and common living areas.
  • Address inside-empty-room details when included in the agreed scope.
  • Help reset visible areas before moving in or after moving out.
  • Plan extra time for buildup, large homes, or heavily used spaces.

Custom Cleaning Plans

Not every household needs the same cleaning routine. Custom cleaning plans allow priorities to be shaped around the way the home is used. A customer may want kitchen and bathroom focus, rotating deep-clean details, guest-room preparation, floor-heavy service, or support before a gathering.

  • Choose room priorities based on the household's most-used spaces.
  • Adjust recurring visits around seasons, guests, pets, or changing routines.
  • Combine standard upkeep with periodic deeper detail work.
  • Set expectations for what can be completed within the scheduled time.

Cleaning Supplies Provided

Shine Time Cleaners brings its own cleaning supplies for appointments. This helps keep the process simple for customers and supports consistency across service visits. If your home has a special surface, sensitive material, or preferred product requirement, note that before service so the cleaning plan can account for it.
