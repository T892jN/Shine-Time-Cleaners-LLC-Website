Pricing

Honest pricing guidance while rates are being finalized.

Shine Time Cleaners has not published fixed prices yet. Residential cleaning costs depend on the home, the service requested, the cleaning frequency, and the level of detail needed, so this page explains the factors that typically shape an estimate without presenting made-up rates.

What affects pricing?

Residential cleaning is influenced by three primary factors: home size, service requested, and cleaning frequency. A compact home needing recurring standard cleaning usually requires less time than a larger home needing a first-time deep cleaning. Condition, number of bathrooms, flooring type, room count, clutter level, pets, special surface instructions, and move-related needs can also influence the final scope.

Home Size

Square footage, number of rooms, number of bathrooms, stairs, flooring types, and the amount of surface area all affect the time required. A larger home may need a team visit or a longer appointment to clean thoroughly.

Service Requested

Standard cleaning, deep cleaning, room-specific service, move-related cleaning, and custom plans require different levels of work. A kitchen-heavy deep cleaning will not be priced the same way as a light maintenance visit.

Cleaning Frequency

Recurring service can keep a home closer to maintenance condition, which may reduce the amount of time required per visit. One-time cleanings often require more resetting, especially when a home has not been professionally cleaned recently.

Why fixed prices are not listed yet

Publishing a fixed price before rates are finalized could create the wrong expectation. Two homes with the same square footage may need very different levels of attention. One may be lightly used and easy to maintain, while another may have heavy kitchen buildup, several bathrooms, pet hair, cluttered surfaces, or move-out details. Shine Time Cleaners will publish clearer pricing information when the company is ready to stand behind those numbers.

Supplies
