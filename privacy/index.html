Privacy Policy

How this website handles privacy, cookies, analytics, and ads.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Shine Time Cleaners website may collect, use, and protect information when visitors browse the site. This policy is written for a small residential cleaning business website and should be reviewed when contact forms or additional tools are added.

Effective date: July 20, 2026

1. Overview

Shine Time Cleaners respects visitor privacy. This website provides information about residential cleaning services, pricing factors, frequently asked questions, contact availability, and company policies. The site may use standard web technologies, including cookies, analytics tools, server logs, and advertising services such as Google AdSense.

The company does not currently publish a phone number or email address on this website, and online contact options are being finalized. If contact forms, booking tools, or customer portals are added later, this policy should be updated to describe the information collected through those features.

2. Information the website may collect automatically

When visitors use the website, technical information may be collected automatically by web hosting services, analytics providers, browser tools, security systems, and advertising partners. This information may include IP address, device type, browser type, operating system, referring page, pages viewed, approximate location derived from IP address, dates and times of visits, and interactions with website content.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small files stored on a visitor's device. This website may use cookies, local storage, pixels, tags, or similar technologies to support site functionality, remember preferences, measure traffic, improve performance, protect the site, and support advertising. Visitors can usually adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies, although some site features may not work as intended if cookies are disabled.

4. Analytics

The website may use analytics tools to understand how visitors find and use the site. Analytics helps identify popular pages, technical issues, general traffic trends, and content that may need improvement. Analytics information is generally aggregated and is not intended to identify individual visitors by name.

5. Google AdSense and advertising

This website may display advertisements through Google AdSense or similar advertising services. Google and its partners may use cookies or identifiers to serve ads, measure ad performance, limit repeated ads, and personalize advertising where permitted by law and user settings. Visitors may be able to manage personalized advertising through Google ad settings and browser privacy controls.

Third-party advertising partners may collect information directly from a visitor's browser or device when ads are displayed. Shine Time Cleaners does not control every cookie or identifier used by third-party advertising networks.

6. Information visitors choose to provide

The current website does not publish a contact form, phone number, or email address. If future online contact options are added, visitors may choose to provide information such as name, service interest, home size, cleaning frequency, preferred timing, and other details relevant to residential cleaning. Any information voluntarily submitted would be used to respond to the request, evaluate service availability, and provide customer support.

7. How information may be used

  • To operate, maintain, secure, and improve the website.
  • To understand visitor interest in services, pricing, FAQ content, and contact availability.
  • To measure website performance and diagnose technical issues.
  • To support advertising, including Google AdSense, where enabled.
  • To respond to requests if contact options are added in the future.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect business rights.

8. Sharing of information

Shine Time Cleaners may share limited information with service providers that help operate the website, such as hosting providers, analytics services, security tools, advertising partners, and technical support providers. These providers may process information according to their own policies and contractual obligations. Information may also be disclosed if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the business, visitors, or others.

9. Data retention

Automatically collected website data may be retained for as long as needed for analytics, security, advertising, legal compliance, and business purposes. Retention periods vary depending on the tool, provider, and type of information. If future contact forms are added, submitted information should be retained only as long as reasonably necessary to handle service inquiries and business records.

10. Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures should be used to protect website information. No website, server, network, or online service can guarantee absolute security. Visitors should avoid sending sensitive personal, financial, medical, or security information through general website tools unless a secure and appropriate channel is provided.

11. Children's privacy

This website is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. Shine Time Cleaners does not knowingly collect personal information from children through this website. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information, they should use the available contact method once published to request review or deletion.

12. Visitor choices

Visitors can manage cookies through browser settings, device settings, privacy extensions, and applicable advertising preference tools. Blocking cookies may affect analytics, personalization, advertising measurement, and some website functionality. Visitors may also use browser features such as private browsing or tracking protection.

13. Third-party links

The website may link to third-party websites or services. Shine Time Cleaners is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party sites. Visitors should review the privacy policies of any third-party services they choose to use.

14. Regional privacy rights

Depending on where a visitor lives, privacy laws may provide rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or opt out of certain uses of personal information. Because this website currently does not publish a direct contact method, privacy request procedures should be added when official contact options are finalized.

15. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the website changes, when new contact options are added, when advertising or analytics tools change, or when legal requirements evolve. The effective date should be revised when material updates are made.

16. Contact status

Shine Time Cleaners does not currently publish a phone number or email address on this website. Online contact options are being finalized. Visitors should check the contact page for the latest status.
